Extra-Credit Assignment

The point of this assignment is two-fold:

  • To give you experience attempting to convince recalcitrant web page owners to fix problems in their page(s).

  • To collectively attempt to clean up De Anza's web site as much as possible.

What you need to do for this assignment is:

  • Contact the owner(s) of De Anza web pages with requests to fix whatever bugs you have noted and filed in our landfill/testopia_tip Bugzilla. You can make this contact via a phone call, an email, or in person.

    Note that the above means that you should NOT contact web page owners regarding bugs filed by other students in the course. Nor should you contact web page owners for page(s) assigned to other students in the course for their test plans. However, this still leaves you with hundreds of pages from which to choose, in addition to the page(s) assigned to you for your own test plan.

  • Feel free to Cc: me on messages sent to web page owners or to provide my contact info to web page owners. If the web page owners realize you are making your request as part of an actual course assignment, they may actually be more responsive than they would be otherwise.

  • Document all of your contacts (and results) in an email message sent to me (mamp@earthlink.net) no later than Tuesday, March 18th. The Subject line should be EXTRA-CREDIT ASSIGNMENT. Make sure your real name as it appears in college records is at the top of the write-up.

  • You will be graded based on your efforts, not your results. The assignment will earn between 1 and 5 points to be added to your overall grade. For example, if your overall grade was a 79 (C+) and your Extra-Credit assignment earned the maximum 5 points, your overall grade would then be raised to an 84 (B). This is by far the biggest opportunity you're going to have to earn extra points, so I encourage you to make the most of it! Go bug those bug-owners now! ;-)
