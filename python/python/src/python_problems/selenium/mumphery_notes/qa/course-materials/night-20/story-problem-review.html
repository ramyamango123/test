Fall Quarter 2007 - DeAnza College

Story Problem Review

  1. Suppose a project team "seeds" their software with 100 bugs, 60 of which are found by the testers. If the testers also find 450 "real" bugs, what is the estimate of "real" bugs which have not been found?

    1. 40
    2. 150
    3. 300
    4. 750
    5. None of the above

  2. Suppose a project had 500 bugs found during the various testing phases, and another 125 found by customers during the first year after the software was released. What would be the DRE for this project?

    1. 20%
    2. 25%
    3. 33%
    4. 80%
    5. None of the above

  3. What is the PhAge of a bug introduced during the Coding phase of a project, but not found until the Integration Testing phase?

    1. 0
    2. 1
    3. 2
    4. 3
    5. 4

  4. What is the PhAge of a bug introduced during the Requirements phase of a project, and caught later in that same phase?

    1. 0
    2. 1
    3. 2
    4. 3
    5. 4

  5. Suppose a project contained 25,000 lines of code, and 375 bugs were found during testing. What is the defect density for this project?

    1. 1.5%
    2. 15%
    3. 33.33%
    4. 66.66%
    5. 85%

  6. If a tester worked 185 hours during a 4-week period, and experienced 37 hours of interrupts during that period, what would his or her E-Factor be?

    1. 0%
    2. 20%
    3. 25%
    4. 80%
    5. None of the above
